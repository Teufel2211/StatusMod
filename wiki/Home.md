# StatusMod Wiki

StatusMod adds lightweight status tags for players on Fabric servers and clients.

This wiki bundle focuses on the supported Fabric build paths:

- Minecraft `1.19` through `1.21.11`
- Dedicated Fabric `26.1`, `26.1.1`, and `26.1.2`

## Quick Summary

- Players can set a custom status and color.
- Status appears in the player list and name rendering.
- Status data is stored locally in `config/statusmod/`.
- Builds are handled by the Fabric multiversion script and the dedicated 26.1 module.
